Avocado Toast Recipe

Fresh, and endlessly customizable, this avocado french toast is the perfect balance of rich flavor and simple ingredients. Smooth mashed avocado sits on top of warm, crispy bread. A sprinkle of salt and pepper brightens each bite, which optional toppings like a drizzle of olive oil, Pico de Galo, or Red Onions add layers of flavor. Light, nutritious, and ready in minutes, it is the ideal breakfast or snack for any day of the week.

Prep: 5 Minutes Cook Time: 0 Minutes Total Time: 5 Minutes

Ingredients Needed

  • 1 Piece Thick Cut Grainy Bread
  • 1/2 Large Ripe Avocado
  • 1-2 Teaspoons Freshly Squeezed Lemon Juice
  • Kosher Salt and Freshly Ground Black Pepper To Taste

Variants

Tomato Basil Toast

  • 1/4 Halved Cherry Tomatoes
  • 2 Tablespoons Basil Vinaigrette

Avacado Toast with Smoked Salmon and Red Onions

  • 2 Thin Sliced Smoked Salmon
  • 2 Tablespoons Thinly Sliced Red Onions

Avocado Toast with Bacon and Eggs

  • 1 Egg Scrambled
  • 1 Teaspoon Freshly Snipped Chives

Avocado Toast with Pico de Gallo

  • 1/4 Cup Pico de Gallo

Directions

  1. Toast the piece of bread until the bread is just golden brown, about 2-3 minutes depending on the thickness of the bread.
  2. For the halved avocado, remove the pit from the avocado and discard. Remove the avocado from the skin and place the avocado flesh into a medium bowl and mash it with a fork and a squeeze of the fresh lemon juice. Season with salt and pepper as needed.
  3. Slater the mashed avocado on top of the toast. Add more salt and pepper is desired and serve immediately.

Directions for Variants

For the Tomato Basil Toast

  1. Toss together the tomatoes and basil vinaigrette in a small bowl. Pile on top of the avocado toast and serve.

Avocado Toast with Smoked Salmon and Red Onions

  1. Lay the smoked salmon on top of the avocado toast, followed by the red onions and serve.

Avocado Toast with Bacon and Eggs

  1. Transfer the scrambled eggs on top of the avocado toast and add the bacon.

Avocado Toast with Goat Cheese and Chives

  1. Sprinkle the goat cheese and chives on top of the avocado toast and serve.

Avocado Toast with Pico de Gallo

  1. Sprinkle the pico de gallo on top of the avocado toast and serve.
