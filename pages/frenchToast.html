French Toast Recipe

Golden, soft, and lightly sweet, this French Toast turns simple bread into a warm, comforting breakfast classic. Each slice is soaked in a rich custard of eggs, milk, and vanilla, then cooked to a perfectly crisp exterior with a tender, fluffy center.A dusting of powdered sugar, a drizzle of syrup, or a handful of fresh fruit adds just the right finishing touch. Cozy, elegant, and always satisfying, this French Toast is a morning treat everyone will love.

Prep: 10 Minutes Cook Time: 20 Minutes Total Time: 30 Minutes

Ingredients Needed

  • 1/4 Cup All Purpose Flour
  • 1 Cup Milk
  • 3 Large Eggs
  • 1 Tablespoon White Sugar
  • 1 Teaspoon Vanilla Extract
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Ground Cinnamon
  • 1 Pinch Salt
  • 12 Slices of Bread

Directions

  1. Place flour into a large mixing bowl; slowly whisk in milk until smooth. Whisk in eggs, sugar, vanilla, cinnamon, and salt until well combined.
  2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium heat. Meanwhile, soak bread slices in milk mixture until saturated.
  3. Working in batches, cook bread on the preheated griddle or pan until golden brown on both sides, about 2 minutes per side.
  4. Serve and Enjoy!
